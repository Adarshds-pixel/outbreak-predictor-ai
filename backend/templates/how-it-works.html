How The AI Outbreak Predictor Works

1. Overview

This AI system predicts **water-borne and infectious disease outbreaks** using environmental, water quality, epidemiological, and social signals. It is built for Smart India Hackathon problem statement: SIH25001 – Smart Community Health Monitoring & Early Warning System.

2. Input Parameters (12 Features)

  • Water Quality (5): pH, Turbidity, Contamination Index, TDS, Water Temperature
  • Environmental (3): Temperature, Humidity, Rainfall
  • Epidemiological (2): Population Density, Cases Last Week
  • Social Indicators (2): Sentiment Score, Resource Utilization

4. Why Prediction Works

  • pH & Turbidity show contamination level
  • Rainfall & humidity show environmental spread risk
  • Cases Last Week shows outbreak momentum
  • Sentiment captures complaints about water quality

5. Weekly Trend Tracking

The dashboard tracks the last 7 predictions to reveal hidden outbreak patterns. You can observe:

  • Increasing risk due to rainfall
  • Sudden spikes in water contamination
  • Slow improvements after public health interventions
